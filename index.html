Set the conditions. Behavior organizes itself.

Lobster Computer is Offline Ventures' engineering studio. We build the software.

What we believe

Intelligent software doesn't get scripted from the top down. You set the rules it runs under and let the behavior organize itself, the way living systems do. We build small pieces, and we don't presume to know how people will combine them.

What we're building

Personal agents are the concrete expression of this. One per person, acting on their behalf. The software should fit a person's life, not the other way around.
