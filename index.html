How we build

Behavior designed top-down is brittle. We set the conditions and let structure emerge from how the pieces connect. That's how markets and ant colonies organize, with no planner required.

Where it goes

It starts with one persistent agent per person. That agent learns you and earns more responsibility as trust builds. Over time, those agents form communities and talk to each other on your behalf.
