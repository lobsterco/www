How we work

Behavior isn't scripted top-down. We build small pieces, write the rules they run under, and watch useful structure fall out of how they interact. We're our own first users.

What we're building

The work right now is personal agents: software that acts on your behalf, one per person. We ship pieces that other developers and agents can build on.
