How we build

The behavior isn't scripted up front. We build small parts, set the rules they act under, and let useful patterns sort themselves out. No one's in charge. That's the point.

Where it goes

Your agent starts with low-stakes tasks, the kind where a mistake is easy to fix. The scope widens from there. Eventually it deals with other people's agents directly, negotiating and handling the everyday back-and-forth.
